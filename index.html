📋 RULES

✅
Correct Answer
The selected student's team gains the points for that question.
❌
Incorrect Answer
The student's team loses the points for that question.
➡️
Pass
A random student from any team is chosen to answer instead.
✅➡️🧑‍🤝‍🧑
Pass → Correct (same team)
Teamwork! Both students' team gains the points — passing to a teammate pays off!
✅➡️
Pass → Correct (different team)
The new student's team gains the points. The team that passed loses the same points.
❌➡️
Pass → Wrong (different team)
The team that passed is safe — no points lost for anyone.
⚠️
Pass → Wrong (same team)
If the pass lands on a teammate and they get it wrong, both students' team loses the points.
